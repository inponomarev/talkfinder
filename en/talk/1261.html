Mobius 2019 SPb (22.05.2019 — 23.05.2019)

Peace and harmony: Android and neural networks

Vyacheslav Tarasov

We’ll discuss on-device CNN launch on Android devices, testing on smartphones, current SDKs, possibility of model conversion for different SDKs, pre- and post-processing of images.

We’ll discuss the following topics:

  • on-device CNN launch on Android devices;

  • testing on smartphones;

  • current SDKs;

  • possibility of model conversion for different SDKs;

  • pre- and post-processing of images: what’s the best way to prepare images for neural networks.

We’ll launch a neural network on Tensorflow (CPU), SNPE by Qualcomm, MACE by Mi, HaAi by Huawei, etc. We’ll look how the network works, how it influences quality and speed.

This talk’s aimed at beginner Android developers who decide to use trained neural networks in their software.
