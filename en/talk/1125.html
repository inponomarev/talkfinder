Vadim will tell how they at Mail.Ru Group approach unit testing for their projects. He will cover not only how they do it, but also how they view it. One of this talk’s ideas is that for programmers unit testing is primarily an architectural approach, not the way to check if the code is correct, i.e. not testing in and of itself.
