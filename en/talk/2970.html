Workshop. Pose recognition: Kamasutra with CameraX

Denis Neklyudov

CameraX goes to release, which means it’s time to add it to our projects, along with face and pose recognition.

No application with authorization and user photos now can do without face recognition on the device.

Denis has been working with authorization and user identification problems for several years. The development of camera features and camera support is part of his job.

The plan: during the workshop, we will add a Camera X library to the application on Android, which not only knows how to take photos and shoot videos, but also recognizes faces, facial expressions, texts in photos, and the human skeleton and his poses.

Workshop requirements: a working environment for Android development and the ability to clone a public GitHub repository.
