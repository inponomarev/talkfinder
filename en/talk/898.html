Heisenbug 2018 SPb (17.05.2018 — 18.05.2018)

Testing to the last: smart responsive interface design patterns

Vitaly Friedman

A microscopic examination of common interface components and problems appearing and tested in responsive user interfaces.

In this session, Vitaly will be taking a microscopic examination of common interface components and problems appearing and tested in responsive user interfaces. We’ll look into what makes a perfect accordion, date/time pickers, feature comparison, insurance calculator and a car configurator, among other things.

Beware: you might not be able to unlearn the things you’ll learn in this session.
