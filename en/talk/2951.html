Heisenbug 2021 SPb (06.04.2021 — 09.04.2021)

Workshop: IDE to help the test engineer (part 2)

Yuri Artamonov

During this workshop, we will figure out where to click, where to twist, and how to bend IntelliJ IDEA to increase our productivity and discuss various tricks when working in the IDE.

A modern IDE is a quite big thing that has been developed for the needs of developers, and sometimes it seems that no one thought about testing engineers. But if you look closely, the needs of QA specialists are not that different from those of developers.

During this workshop, we will figure out where to click, where to twist, and how to bend IntelliJ IDEA to increase our productivity and discuss various tricks when working in the IDE.
