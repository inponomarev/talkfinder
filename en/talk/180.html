HolyJS 2019 Msc (08.11.2019 — 09.11.2019)

Soft Effect: Skills development research

Andrew Smirnov

During this session Andrew Smirnov would like to share his thoughts about frontend, what skills developer should learn and why soft skills are more important now.

In 2019 researches realized that soft skills are more important for developers career than hard skills. In the next few months a study was conducted to analyze the importance of skills and the dynamics of the impact of their growth. The research was based on the statistics providing an interesting evidential base.

They called it the greatest discovery in development history.

Some researches call it Soft Effect.
