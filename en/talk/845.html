The epic battle: iOS vs. Android

Yonatan Levin

Let’s prove together which platform has better developers!

There is no such thing as a bad operating system. It’s all about the developers who create an application for their users. It’s all about their knowledge.

Who is the most knowledgeable community?

Is it Android developers who need to balance between insane OS fragmentation or iOS dev who need to align with strict Apple guidelines?

The first time in history, you have the power to prove which platform has better developers.

How?

Come to our closing ~keynote~ "experiment" and you will figure it out.
