Heisenbug 2020 Msc (04.11.2020 — 07.11.2020)

SberProfi community: Standards and game rules

Mikhail Rogov, Ivan Kravtsov

<p>Ivan and Mikhail will discuss:</p> <ul> <li>Agile transformation: scale and numbers;</li> <li>how QA engineers live in product teams and how to create uniform working rules of the game without reinventing the wheel;</li> <li>how the community works and how it should influence the rules of the game.</li> </ul>
