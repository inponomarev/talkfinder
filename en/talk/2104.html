Evaluation of the mobile project: How not to scare the client and not going down the tube

Maksim Leykin

In this talk Maksim wants to share his experience in assessing the complexity of the project and tell how to conduct an objective and high-quality assessment quickly and painlessly.

For more than 7 years Maksim has regularly been involved in the assessment of the complexity of mobile application development projects and has verified colleagues' assessments. During that time there were, of course, both successful cases and failures.

By analyzing both of them Maksim gradually came to understand what factors are most important to consider when making assessments, what mistakes we often make, and how these errors affect the final result.

In this talk speaker wants to share his experience and remove some "rakes" from your path in order to get to the goal i.e. objective and high-quality assessment of the project, quickly and painlessly.
