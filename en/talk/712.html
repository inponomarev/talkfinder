Heisenbug 2017 Msc (08.12.2017 — 09.12.2017)

Testers' classification from a developer point of view

Mikalai Alimenkou

We’ll show you an amusing testers' classification so that you could consider the way people see you and perhaps change the situation for the better.

Testers often talk about their confrontation and conflicts with developers. There are some teams though that manage to live in peace and harmony. So, maybe something’s wrong? We would like to talk about the way developers see all testers. We’ll show you an amusing classification that includes a fly-tester, a nazi-tester, a panda-tester and other characters. That’s an opportunity for you to think on the way people see you and perhaps to change the situation for the better.

This talk will be interesting for project managers and team leads as well. You’ll be able to recognize various patterns of testers' behaviour and take actions to increase your teamwork level. See you at the conference!
