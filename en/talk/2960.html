Mobius 2021 SPb (13.04.2021 — 16.04.2021)

Graphics optimization on Metal

Georgy Ostrobrod

The talk is about the optimization of applications using Apple Metal.

During this session, you will learn about methods of profiling and optimization of graphics on Metal, possible weak points and their overcoming, optimization of data preparation, rendering and shaders, use of compute-shaders, and their optimization.
