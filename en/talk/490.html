Measuring UX in an iOS app

Alexander Zimin

The ways you can measure the user interaction within your app, and the reasons you need to.

Developers are afraid of swizzling and for that reason they avoid extending the existing components of UIKit. Which is a pity because doing it can tell a lot about your users: for example, do they miss when tapping on a button, or what part of a table do they spend the most time in.

This talk examines the ways you can measure the user interaction within the app, and why do you need it to begin with.
