Deep diving into the Kotlin Coroutines API

Filip BabiÄ‡

In this talk, we’ll go over the Kotlin Coroutines API in depth, see what the underlying structures and mechanisms are, and how you can build your own extensions to these mechanisms.

Have you been learning Kotlin Coroutines by trial and error? Do you need a change of pace and environment or just a guide which doesn’t baffle you with the sheer amounts of computer science terms, which coroutines are built upon?

Look no more! In this session, we’ll go over the majority of the Kotlin Coroutines API, with the emphasis on explaining how each component works, which principles and elements are used behind the scenes, and how you can utilize Coroutines in your code, without burning out due to all the theory that is the foundation of computing and multi-threading.
