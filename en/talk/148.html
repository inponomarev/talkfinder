DotNext 2019 Msc (06.11.2019 — 07.11.2019)

Pragmatic memory management

Anatoly Zhmur

Anatoly will talk about the principles of working with CG and how to improve your performance.

When talking about GC a lot of time is traditionally devoted to its device and abstract tips which rarely help to improve something besides abstract memory traffic metrics. We will try to consider this from the practical point of view of the engineer, from performance measures to concepts underlying assembly waste. There will be a little provocation.
