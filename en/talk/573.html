DevOops 2017 (20.10.2017 — 20.10.2017)

10 reasons [not] to use k8s

Ivan Glushkov

We’ll dwell on the problems that we encountered while working with k8s, give some advice that helped us, and try to prove that in the modern world you can’t live without k8s.

Based on personal experience we’ll show the problems that we encountered while working with k8s, and give some advice that helped us and most likely will help you. We’ll try to prove that in the modern world you can’t live without k8s. For those who don’t like k8s, we’ll provide some proofs why you shouldn’t move to it.
