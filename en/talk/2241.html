JPoint 2020 (29.06.2020 — 03.07.2020)

When it all went to Kafka 2: Disperse producers

Gregory Koshelev

A talk about Producer tuning performance: finding pinch points and choosing the definitive characteristics for the requirements.

<p>Continuation of the talk "When it all went to Kafka", but the main attention will get a Producer: we will figure out what is under his hood and how it all works.

This talk will be about Producer tuning performance: finding pinch points and choosing the definitive characteristics for the requirements.</p> <p>We will analyze the work of the Producer:</p> <ul> <li>What settings affect their performance;</li> <li>What performance metrics we have and what do they mean;</li> <li>A search of pinch points;</li> <li>Selection of characteristics for external requirements.</li> </ul>
