DotNext 2017 SPb (19.05.2017 — 20.05.2017)

Interprocess communications: reasons and approaches

Igor Labutin

We will see why the need to communicate between processes might arise, will discuss how to solve the problem of communication and will find out that some methods are better and more convenient than others.

The amount of applications that are stepping outside simple approach «use only one process» is growing. Developers find a lot of reasons why one process is not enough anymore. And as soon as there are multiple processes involved the problem of communication arises: how to pass information between them. .NET offers several means to implement such communication.

In this talk we will see why the need to communicate between processes might arise, will discuss how to solve the problem of communication and will find out that some methods are better and more convenient than others.
