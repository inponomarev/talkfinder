Mobius 2018 SPb (20.04.2018 — 21.04.2018)

Services: to live with and without

Yonatan Levin

We’ll explore what has changed, what changes you as a developer require to make to leave in a new world of Android background restrictions and why da hell this is important.

Every new Android release breath a change. The wild west of endless running threads is over. No more smoking barrels of "last 5% battery". You require to act and seriously consider how you became the excellent citizen of Android device and play side by side with a marshall law of background tasks. Or die trying.

At this talk, we will explore what has changed, what changes you as a developer require to make to leave in a new world of background restrictions and why da hell this is important. It will not be a talk where you learn how to do X by applying Y. It’s a talk where you will require to think, ask questions and follow the white rabbit hole :)
