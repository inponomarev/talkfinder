Mobius 2018 Msc (08.12.2018 — 09.12.2018)

SceneKit in use

Vyacheslav Gilevich

Vyacheslav will tell how to start working with SceneKit, which pitfalls they encountered and how to record video alongside rendering in SceneKit and ARKit working. He will also tell about little-knows features of this engine and how you can use them to implement the most complex visual effects.

In 2012 Apple introduced SceneKit, their own 3D engine, and two years later ported it to iOS. Yandex.Maps team use it to show routes and objects in augmented reality.

Vyacheslav will tell how to start working with this engine, which pitfalls they encountered and how to record video alongside rendering in SceneKit and ARKit working. He will also tell about little-knows features of this engine and how you can use them to implement the most complex visual effects.
