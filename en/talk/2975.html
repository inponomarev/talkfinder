Mobius 2021 SPb (13.04.2021 — 16.04.2021)

Developer-tutor. Should you teach?

Ekaterina Bateeva

Ekaterina Bateeva will tell you whether it makes sense for a developer to go into teaching.

For the past few years, Ekaterina has taught on many platforms and in different formats. During this session, she wants to talk about whether it makes sense for a developer to go into teaching.

The speaker will talk about difficulties and how to avoid them, as well as what bonuses it will bring and what skills can be acquired. The talk will be useful for specialists of any level.
