TechTrain 2019 (24.08.2019 — 25.08.2019)

(In)adequate technical interview

Anton Arhipov

In this talk we will discuss what questions you encounter when hiring and whether these questions are adequate at all.

Community is dapple with stories about questions asked during technical interviews. Yet these stories are also accompanied by an emotion outburst: "I’ve been flooded! They are all ghouls, ask some strange questions! What do they want from me?!" The same stories by employ look like "You can’t find people! They can’t count two and two! I should find someone, work doesn’t go!"

In this talk we will discuss what questions you encounter during an interview, are they adequate at all and most importantly, who deal with questions you don’t know how to answer.
