TechTrain 2019 (24.08.2019 — 25.08.2019)

AI vs. AI

Grigori Bakunov

Many people are interested in security problems of AI and how easy it’s to deceive him, especially now, when attacks are constantly improving. Grigori will answer all questions on the subject of errors and manipulations of artificial intelligence.

All sorts of approaches to AI appears more and more often, today we hear about new projects every day, in which AI will replace people. But is it safe? How much artificial intelligence is protected from errors and malicious manipulations.

In his talk, Grigory will tell about how it’s simple to cheat the typical AI and what errors it may cause. Also, we will look at a few examples of how specific attacks on AI affected (or could have) the life of a person or society.
