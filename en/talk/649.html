Mastering GPU engineering in one hour

Andrey Volodin

What you can do with GPU on your mobile device. Talk pays attention to modern trending technologies such as neural networks.

GPU is the key thing that empowers most of world-changing technologies we currently see: bitcoin, machine learning, AR/VR, CGI â€” all of them are executed via graphics cards.

However, it is uncommon among iOS developers to incorporate GPU into their development pipeline. Some of them simply don’t need it, others either think it is too complicated or don’t even know there’s GPU in iPhone.

This talk will give you a picture of what you can do with GPU on your mobile device. And of course we’re going to pay attention to modern trending technologies such as neural networks.
