Heisenbug 2019 Msc (05.12.2019 — 06.12.2019)

We have DevOps. No one was fired. But what to do?!

Baruch Sadogursky

In his second talk from "the DevOps" series, Baruch will tell about the necessity for digital transformation and give detailed information with instructions on what to do and how to negotiate with business and testers.

So, last year we figured out that no one is getting fired. Phew, that’s good. But we also figured that if your business is not transforming, it will fall behind and become irrelevant. That’s bad. So, what can we do?

In the talk Baruch will explain what to learn, what to say, and what to do when the argument "this is how they do it in Netflix" doesn’t work.

We’ll discuss the required reading list, who to talk to (who are those mythical "stakeholders" and why are they holding those steaks? Is it a meat factory transformation thing?), and what to say: how to go about digital transformation for the sake of the future of the business in general, and the testers in particular.
