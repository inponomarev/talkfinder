DevOops 2020 Msc (06.07.2020 — 10.07.2020)

How to make a DevOps tool and turn it into a business in the USA with clients and investors

Matvey Kukui

Matvey will talk about how DevOps tools turn into a product business in his own example. And what you need to learn with an engineering background.

Amixr Inc. is a startup based in San Francisco with a dozen employees, investment, product, and sales. A half and a year ago it was a strange idea of an ordinary DevOps engineer.

Matvey will talk about how DevOps tools turn into a product business in his own example. And what you need to learn with an engineering background.
