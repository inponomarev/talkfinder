Heisenbug 2021 SPb (06.04.2021 — 09.04.2021)

Mobile phone testing — new way. Deep dive into HW stands

Nadezhda Davydova

Issues at the border between SW and HW — creating and automating stands for complex testing of new operating system for mobile phones.

During this session you will learn about:

  • developing and choosing approaches to testing a mobile phone with a new operating system, ways of testing;

  • deep functional testing of mobile phones by modelling a wide range of complex network operating modes using emulator of mobile communication tower;

  • automated testing using QEMU virtualization and QMP library;

  • approach to building automated HW stands.

Technologies:

  • SCPI;

  • adb;

  • QEMU virtualization + QMP management;

  • pytest, Python;

  • Arduino (will be briefly mentioned).

Target audience: testers and developers.
