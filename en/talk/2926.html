Heisenbug 2021 SPb (06.04.2021 — 09.04.2021)

Pitfalls in testing projects with computer vision

Sofia Selezneva, Andrey Vavilin

Sofia and Andrey will talk about the intricacies of testing projects with computer vision.

Technologies of computer vision step by step sneak into our world. Mobile apps, video and image editors, security systems use computer vision. At the same time, this topic remains pretty new from the point of development and testing and requires specific knowledge. Sofia and Andrey will tell what is computer vision and what it is used for. They will view testing projects with computer vision from two points: development and testing.
