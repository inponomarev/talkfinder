Mobius 2021 SPb (13.04.2021 — 16.04.2021)

How not to write a pirate ship instead of a frigate and vice versa

Igor Karenkov

In this session, Igor would like to compare the effectiveness of the famous principles and practices in Android depending on what project we are developing.

On Mobius we often hear about cool solutions from large teams that may not be good within small teams and projects.

In this session, Igor would like to compare the effectiveness of the famous principles and practices in Android (SOLID, Clean, Modularization, Architecture, etc.) depending on what project we are developing so everyone can better understand certain engineering practices.
