DotNext 2020 Msc (02.12.2020 — 05.12.2020)

Domain-driven design distilled

Vladimir Khorikov

The talk about main Domain-driven design principles.

Vladimir would like to show the most important parts of DDD in his opinion with examples in C#. The goal is to give an overview of the main things from DDD, so that the viewer can quickly begin to navigate in them and put them into practice.
