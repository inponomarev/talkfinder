Making your microscope for iOS application testing

Victoria Kashlina, Anton Vlasov

Anton and Victoria will talk about the experience of using Xcode Instruments and how it came in handy during the developing of the Sberbank Online application.

Debugging and testing are inherent parts of developers life. But only a few people know about new tools provided by Apple to modernize this process â€” signpost is just one of these examples.

For Sberbank Online project uses similar tools that allow you to find thin parts in the code’s work and make the best decision for its improvement. The next step was the creation of a custom tool that allowed to debug external framework due to which Sberbank’s team wrote unit tests.
