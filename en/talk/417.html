IMDG/GridGain, industrial application

Mikhail Getmanov, Dmitry Drozhzhin

Examining specifics of client data storage in new Sberbank IT platform.

This talk we’ll examine specifics of client data storage in new Sberbank IT platform, discuss nuances of integration with the existing infrastructure and share experience of subtle grid tuning for achieving the best performance possible.
