Heisenbug 2019 SPb (17.05.2019 — 18.05.2019)

Recon techniques in 2k19

Igor Lyrchikov

Igor will describe in detail how recon’s being performed, covering procedures of gathering information about a test object, ways of automating these actions on any scripting language and existing solutions which are mainly used in the industry.

Recon is the first step for every hacker or pentester; Igor will describe in detail how it’s being performed. He’ll cover procedures of gathering information about a test object, ways of automating these actions on any scripting language and existing solutions which are mainly used in the industry.

This talk’s aimed at everyone who’d like to know what pentesters do and what they begin their working process with. The audience will learn what could become the base of cyberattack on their product or company.
