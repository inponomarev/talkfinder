HolyJS 2019 SPb (24.05.2019 — 25.05.2019)

Modern web testing and automation with Puppeteer

Andrey Lushnikov

The future of web testing and automation belongs to Puppeteer — Andrey will show why and how.

Andrey will explain how Puppeteer works and what makes it so fast. He’ll show interesting use cases and will discuss how Puppeteer fits into clouds and docker containers. The future of web testing and automation belongs to Puppeteer — Andrey will show why and how.
