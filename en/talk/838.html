What mom never told you about multithreading

Fernando Cejas

We’ll learn about different ways to handle, manage and master multithreading on mobile platforms.

When we were little kids, surely there were many taboo topics our moms never mentioned. There is actually a big chance one of those is multithreading.

That is why in this talk we are going to walk together through the different alternatives we have nowadays in order to handle, manage and master multithreading on mobile platforms.

Our focus will be mainly Android but all the content and techniques exposed here can be also used within other platforms for software engineering threading problem solving.
