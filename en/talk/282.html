"Non-mobile" Androids review: TV, Wear, Auto & Things

Fedor Tsymbal

This talk will be about variety of Android operating systems and how to write cross-platform applications for these systems.

Android is becoming more variative. Nominally, now it’s not just one OS, but a whole bouquet â€” Android Things and Android Automotive (and it’s not the same as Android Auto) are added to habitual Android, Android Wear and Android TV. And there are Android One and Android Go too.

This talk is an attempt to understand the variety and systematize it: we’ll do a short review of each system, understand how it differs from the classic Android and try to write cross Android app that will fit all the listed versions.
