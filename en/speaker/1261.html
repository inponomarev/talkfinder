Sofia Selezneva

ABBYY

QA Lead on a machine learning and image analysis project at ABBYY. Before that, she set up the process of testing the video analysis system at ATAPY software from scratch. Sofia has experience working with projects related to computer vision, both in start-up companies and in large companies with an established testing and development system.

  • Testing ML products: Don't turn a black box into a black hole (Heisenbug 2020 SPb, 15.06.2020 — 18.06.2020)

  • Pitfalls in testing projects with computer vision (Heisenbug 2021 SPb, 06.04.2021 — 09.04.2021)
