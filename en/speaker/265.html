Konstantin Goldstein

Microsoft

Technology expert, business consultant with 16 years of experience in IT industry of Israel, the USA and Europe. Konstantin is an author of technological acceleration program of startups and ISVs. He is driving blockchain ecosystem in Central Eastern Europe.
