Tim Hinrichs

Styra, Inc. tlhinrichs timothyhinrichs

Tim Hinrichs is a co-creator of the Open Policy Agent project and the CTO and co-founder of Styra. Before that, he was a PTL in OpenStack and was a software engineer at VMware. Tim spent the last 20 years developing declarative languages for different domains such as cloud-computing, software-defined networking, configuration management, web security, and access-control. He received his PhD in Computer Science from Stanford University in 2008.
