Andrey Kogun

CROC andrei_kogun kogun

Born in Moscow, Andrey has been working in IT for more than 15 years, for the last 6 years - in KROK. During his career he took part in many custom development projects, mainly e-document flow systems and business processes automatization for several big Russian companies as a senior developer and an architect. At the universities of Moscow, he delivers lectures in development basics with the use of Java technologies. Organizer and leader of [Moscow Java developers community](https://jug.msk.ru).

  • Spring Boot and Xtend: black magic session with exposure (JPoint 2018, 06.04.2018 — 07.04.2018)

  • Round table with Joker organizers (Joker 2018, 19.10.2018 — 20.10.2018)

  • Know-how as code: the (java) developer's guide to the code generation (SnowOne 2020, 29.02.2020 — 29.02.2020)
