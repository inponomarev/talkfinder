Yuri Artamonov

JetBrains Yuriy_Artamonov jreznot

For the last 10 years Yuri have been developing libraries, frameworks and tools for developers. As part of academic activities, he mentored applied math students from the Samara University.

Currently, working at IntelliJ IDEA Team of JetBrains and trying to bring new useful tools to developers daily routine. Author of Selenium UI Testing plugin and maintainer of Gauge support for IntelliJ IDEA.
