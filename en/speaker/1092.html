Viktor Orlovsky

Yandex viclovsky

Viktor has been worked in Yandex in automated testing since 2013. He has had a lot of projects in testing REST API services, services that use IMAP/POP3 application layer protocols, web interfaces, and mobile applications. Viktor is one of the founders of the OpenAPI generator and author of swagger-coverage. He runs blog on automated testing.

  • Swagger/OpenAPI Specification as the basis for your acceptance tests (Heisenbug 2020 SPb, 15.06.2020 — 18.06.2020)
