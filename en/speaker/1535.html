Mala Gupta

JetBrains eMalaGupta malagupta

Java Champion

Mala Gupta works as a Developer Advocate with JetBrains. A Java Champion, she has authored multiple books with Manning, Packt, and O'Reilly Publications. She is an international speaker at industry conferences and has over 20 years of experience in the software industry. She has been actively supporting Java certifications as a path to career advancement.

Mala works closely with the developer communities. She co-leads Delhi's Java User Group. A strong supporter of Women in Technology, she also drives initiatives of the Women Who Code Delhi chapter to augment the participation of women in tech.
