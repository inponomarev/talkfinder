Sergey Mahetov

Kontur

Leading tester at Kontur. In the field of software development for more than 10 years, in testing for more than 5. The main direction is the testing and operation of infrastructure solutions. As a rule, these are highly performanced, fault tolerant integration buses, based on various message brokers and information storage systems, as well as on NoSQL databases. Main stack — Linux/JVM, favorite language — Scala, tools — Jmeter/Gatling.
