Pavel Yosifovich

Author of the "Windows Internals" book zodiacon

Pavel Yosifovich is a developer, trainer author and speaker. He's the co-author of "Windows Internals 7th edition Part 1" (2017), author of "WPF 4.5 Cookbook" (2012) and "Windows Kernel Programming" (2019). Pavel is also a former MVP and a Pluralsight author. He can be reached at zodiacon@live.com or through his <a href="http://blogs.microsoft.co.il/pavely"; target="_blank">blog</a>.
