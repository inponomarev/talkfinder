Alexey Potapov

ITMO

Doctor of Science. Professor at the Department of Computer Photonics and Videomatics of ITMO University. 20 years of experience in R&D and academic research projects in the fields of computer vision, pattern recognition, machine learning and artificial general intelligence with more than 130 scientific papers and 3 books. Current fields of interest are universal algorithmic intelligence, cognitive architectures, probabilistic programming and deep learning, especially including neuro-bayesian approach and meta-learning.
