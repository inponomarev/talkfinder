Vitaly Khudobakhshov

JetBrains

khudobakhshov khud

Was born in 1979 in St.Petersburg (Leningrad) and graduated from Physics Department of St.Petersburg State University. His experience with Java dates back from the version 1.1. For many years Vitaly was an assistant professor in St.Petersburg State University and taught computer science and mathematics courses. From 2015 is a senior analyst in Odnoklassniki, where he deals with various aspects of data mining and machine learning. Has been involved with functional programming for many years and outside of the main job and Java stack is also a Haskell programmer. For data mining purposes uses both Spark/Scala and R programming language.
