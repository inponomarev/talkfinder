TensorFlow for mobile devs

Enrique López Mañas

Make your first steps into the fascinating world of ML for mobile.

There is a lot of hype around ML and AI lately, and TensorFlow is the framework of choice from Google. But as a mobile developer you might have asked yourself, how can I benefit from it? This talk will help you make your first steps into the fascinating world of ML for mobile.
